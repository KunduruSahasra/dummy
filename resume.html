Title:REVOLUTIONIZING HEALTH CARE SUPPLY CHAIN TRACEABILITY THROUGH BLOCK CHAIN TECHNOLOGY

Healthcare supply chain involves a lot of complexity. It has a larger impact on human health. One of the major concerns in the current healthcare supply chain is drug counterfeiting. Supply chain management is an activity responsible for moving commodities and services from one point to another through a variety of stakeholders. The traditional SCM is based on a centralized approach managed at the central headquarter, and all other sub-offices get instructions from the main o ice. Some major issues with present supply chain management systems are security, transactional transparency, traceability, stakeholder involvement, product counterfeiting, additional delays, fraud, and instabilities. Therefore, it is very essential for the consumer to know whether the drug has come from the rightful source. So in such scenarios, a blockchain application can be used since it provides the features in such a way that a consumer can track and trace the drug in the supply chain with the help of smart contracts which provides an immutable history of transitions to all the stakeholders thereby eliminating the involvement of unauthorized people. It can also aid in transaction authorization and verification in the supply chain or payments without a third party. So here we described an Ethereum based decentralized Blockchain system with products anti-counterfeiting in such a way that manufacturers can use this system to provide genuine products without having to manage direct-operated stores, which can significantly reduce the cost of product quality assurance. Adding GIS to this proposed system can help to live track accurately.
